Why It Matters

Space decisions affect operations long after the lease is signed.

Good tenant representation is about more than finding options. It is about reducing friction in how the business actually works, whether that means customer access, truck flow, employee convenience, or future expansion flexibility.

FAQ

Tenant representation questions

What does tenant representation include?

Site selection, market evaluation, occupancy cost review, tour strategy, lease negotiation, and guidance on how location affects operations.

Why should a business use tenant representation in Northwest Indiana?

Because different submarkets serve different customer, labor, and access needs, and local guidance helps avoid bad-fit space decisions.

Can tenant representation help retail, office, and industrial users?

Yes. Each use type has different criteria, but all can benefit from better market evaluation and lease negotiation.

What should tenants compare besides rent?

Total occupancy cost, visibility, parking, access, neighboring uses, buildout, logistics fit, and business-operating impact.

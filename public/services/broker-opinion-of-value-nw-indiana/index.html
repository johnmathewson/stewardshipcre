FAQ

BOV questions

What is a broker opinion of value?

A market-based estimate of commercial property value informed by comparables, income analysis, positioning factors, and current demand.

When do owners ask for a BOV?

Before a sale, refinance discussion, partnership decision, estate-planning review, or repositioning conversation.

How is a BOV different from an appraisal?

A BOV is a broker’s market opinion tied to likely pricing and positioning. An appraisal is a different product prepared under a different scope and standard.

Why does local context matter in a Northwest Indiana BOV?

Because corridor differences, taxes, tenant demand, and municipal nuance all influence commercial value in this market.

Buyer Brief

Crown Point property usually works best when the asset fits the city’s next-wave demand instead of just riding the growth narrative.

Buyers need to study access, corridor competition, site timing, and whether the local demand depth truly supports the pricing. Some assets deserve the premium. Others borrow too much from the city’s reputation.

What tends to work

Common mistakes
